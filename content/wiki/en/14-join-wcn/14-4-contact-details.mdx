## Official channels

Trust only the public pages and wiki on **wcn.network**. Policy and process follow the site and official correspondence. Submit every node and cooperation lead through **[wcn.network/apply](https://wcn.network/apply)**; scattered direct messages are not the channel of record.

In the form, give a reachable **email, Telegram, or WeChat**, consistent with your organization name and role, so the team can run due diligence and reply. If an existing node or partner referred you, name the referrer and the relationship in **Why WCN** or the notes field for conflict checks and priority sorting.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Node seat intent" icon="target">Pick the Node type for capital, projects, or services. In Resources and Looking for, record what you deliver and the seat role you seek.</Card>
  <Card title="Institutional cooperation" icon="handshake">Mark the cooperation nature — strategic, service, or regional — in Role or Node type. Put capability and need in the two long fields.</Card>
  <Card title="Project financing need" icon="rocket">Enter the project or holding entity as the organization. Put traction in Resources, and round, amount range, and window in Looking for.</Card>
  <Card title="Media and distribution" icon="megaphone">State your audience, compliance boundaries, and pricing or volume model. Add links to representative work where available.</Card>
</CardGrid>

## What to expect after you submit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Delivery confirmed">The success page returns a reference ID. Save the screenshot or email for your record.</Step>
  <Step title="Sorting and pre-screening">The business side queues your lead by type. If materials are incomplete, it may follow up through the contact you left.</Step>
  <Step title="In-depth conversation">On a fit, the team schedules a call to align mandate, compliance, and the next pilot or seat step.</Step>
  <Step title="No fit or hold">You may receive a courteous explanation or a request for more materials. This does not bar a future application.</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">To move faster, use an institutional email or a real-name account, and avoid handles that cannot be verified. Chinese and English are both accepted; keep one submission in one language to aid sorting.</Callout>

<Statement>The contact page turns reading into a trackable next step. Always enter through the official Apply at wcn.network to open an auditable, peer-to-peer record between WCN and your organization.</Statement>
