## Official contact channels

1. **Website and Information**: Please only trust public pages and Wikis on the domain name **wcn.network**; policies and procedures are subject to the site and official correspondence.  
2. **Node and cooperation intake**: uniformly use **[wcn.network/apply](https://wcn.network/apply)** to submit; scattered private messages are not used as the only acceptance method.  
3. **Contact information in the form**: Be sure to fill in the receivable **email, Telegram or WeChat** (at least one), consistent with the name of the organization and position, to facilitate due diligence and return visits.  
4. **Recommendation**: If introduced by an existing node or partner, write the full name/organization and relationship of the referrer in the **Why WCN** or remarks field to facilitate conflict verification and priority processing.

## What you should expect after submitting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm delivery">The success page prompts that the application has been reviewed; please save the screenshot or email (if it is activated later).</Step>
  <Step title="Sorting and pre-screening">The business side will be queued up by type; if the materials are incomplete, additional inquiries may be made through the **one-way** contact information you left.</Step>
  <Step title="Deep conversation">If the match is successful, a meeting will be scheduled to align Mandate, compliance and the next pilot or seat process.</Step>
  <Step title="No match or hold">You may receive polite explanations or suggestions for supplementary documents; this does not mean that you are prohibited from applying again in the future.</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">To speed up processing: use institutional email or real-name social accounts; avoid leaving anonymous handles that cannot be verified; both Chinese and English are accepted, but please keep the full text in the same language to facilitate sorting.</Callout>

<Statement>The contact page's job is to turn reading into actionable, trackable next steps; please always enter the system through wcn.network's official Apply to establish a peer-to-peer, auditable starting point for communication between WCN and your organization.</Statement>
