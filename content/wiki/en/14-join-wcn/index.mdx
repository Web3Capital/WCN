## A node is a position, not a purchase

A node is an accountable position in the network. It is not an identity bought with a fee. Every holder accepts four duties: bring resources, advance collaboration, submit evidence, and comply.

<Callout type="warning">
WCN is in Phase 1, the Business Network period (M0–M12). Early entrants find seeded deal flow from the founder's pipeline. Non-contributing nodes face review and exit.
</Callout>
