<Hero badge="Chapter 8 · Proof of Business" lead="Proof of Business (PoB) is the proof mechanism for a real, verifiable, traceable business outcome. It does not record who took part. It records who advanced a result that review confirmed. Once reconciled, a PoB record produces a Verification Node on the Proof Ledger.">
# PoB is the settlement gate of WCN. Without a proven outcome, there is no system-level recognition and no settlement.

<Callout type="tip">
Remove PoB, and rewards drift to the most observable proxy — holdings, follower counts, check-ins — decoupled from real output. PoB keeps recognition tied to what gets done, not to how busy a participant appears.
</Callout>
