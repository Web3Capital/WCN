## The four meanings, as a strategic close

The four functions of PoB are not separate features. Together they decide what the network treats as real.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Result threshold" icon="gem">What the system recognizes is not computing power, stake, or check-ins. It is the business final state that PoB Sealed into the ledger.</Card>
  <Card title="Attribution entry" icon="puzzle">A verbal close cannot move across organizations. PoB gives a citable, reviewable record, lowering the cost of repeated trust.</Card>
  <Card title="Settlement precondition" icon="coins">Long-term distribution binds to the Proof Ledger, so honest delivery outpays a farmed proxy. No PoB, no settlement.</Card>
  <Card title="Network memory" icon="brain">Model output is not PoB. Adopted output with a final state is. PoB gives the agent economy an entry aligned with real work, not wash activity.</Card>
</CardGrid>

## The economic flywheel

The flywheel turns because a proven result is reusable, and a reusable record lowers the cost of the next collaboration.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Quality becomes legible">Review and exclusion rules make a good result identifiable, so reputation accrues to the nodes and the paths that produced it.</Step>
  <Step title="Matching gets faster">A new entrant searches the PoB history, shortening diligence and cold start, the way a verifiable track record lowers the bar to trust.</Step>
  <Step title="Settlement stays credible">When long-term distribution binds to the ledger, honest delivery outpays proxy-farming at the margin — unlike a model where the largest holder always wins.</Step>
  <Step title="The pool deepens">More Sealed loops make the network harder to replace, and the resource pool compounds against later entrants.</Step>
</Steps>

The flywheel is not automatic. It rests on review quality, on governance that resists capture, and on attribution fairness (see 8.5).

<Statement>PoB is not a plug-in module of WCN. It is the value definition, the accounting unit, and the entry to long-term value, in one.</Statement>

---

## If PoB were removed

Remove the gate, and the network drifts to whatever is easiest to measure. Each effect compounds the others.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Node meaning dilutes" icon="triangle-alert">Contributions stop being comparable, and relationships compete with publicity — a free-for-all with no shared record.</Card>
  <Card title="Agents stay at the tool level" icon="triangle-alert">Generated and adopted cannot be told apart, so tool budgets struggle to enter settlement.</Card>
  <Card title="Settlement distorts" icon="triangle-alert">Incentives drift to airdrop-style rules, point tasks, and holdings — systematically misaligned with what PoB measures.</Card>
  <Card title="Memory does not accumulate" icon="triangle-alert">Closed loops never settle into composable reputation, and every collaboration restarts from zero trust.</Card>
</CardGrid>

## The whole chain in one line

PoW buys security. PoS buys economic consensus. PoB buys business reality. WCN ties scarce rewards to the third line, so PoB is the core layer, not the copy layer.

<Callout type="tip">
To judge whether WCN is as serious as it claims, read its PoB execution: the rejection rate, the quality of supplements, and whether a Verdict stays open to rule iteration. The system is written in 8.1 through 8.5. The credibility is written in how review runs.
</Callout>
