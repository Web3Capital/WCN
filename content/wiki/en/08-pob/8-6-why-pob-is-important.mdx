## Four core meanings (compressed expression)

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Define value semantics" icon="gem">What the system recognizes is not computing power, pledges or sign-ins, but the final business state archived by PoB - orthogonal to the "security budget" of PoW/PoS.</Card>
  <Card title="From made to proven" icon="circle-check">Oral close cannot be circulated across organizations; PoB provides **quotable and reviewable** public records, reducing the cost of repeated trust.</Card>
  <Card title="Incentive compatibility and the flywheel" icon="repeat">Real closed loop ↑ → Verifiable cases ↑ → New nodes/capital are more willing to access → Deeper resource pool → Closed loop is more difficult to replace. **Comparison**: Airdrop flywheels often stop at the wool party; PoB flywheels are anchored in delivery and acceptance.</Card>
  <Card title="AI enters the value layer" icon="bot">The model output itself is not PoB; **adopted + final state** is. PoB gives the Agent economy an entrance that is aligned with non-washing behavior.</Card>
</CardGrid>

## Economic flywheel (brief description)

1. **Explicit quality standards**: Review and exclusion rules (8.3–8.4) make "good results" identifiable, and reputation capital is accumulated on nodes and paths.
2. **Matching efficiency**: New entrants can search historical PoB, shortening due diligence and cold start, similar to the credibility premium of GIPS compliant funds in the eyes of institutional allocators.
3. **Settlement is credible**: When long-term allocation is linked to the PoB ledger, **marginally** honest delivery is better than brushing the agency indicator - different from the allocation logic of "big accounts always win" under PoS.

The flywheel is not automatic: Rely on Proof Desk quality, governance capture prevention, and attribution fairness (8.5).

<Statement>PoB is not a plug-in module of WCN, but a three-in-one combination of values, accounting units and long-term value portals.</Statement>

## If there is no PoB

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Node semantic dilution" icon="triangle-alert">Contributions are incomparable, returning relationships compete with publicity—similar to the performance publicity free-for-all without GIPS.</Card>
  <Card title="AI stops at the tool level" icon="triangle-alert">It is impossible to distinguish between "generated" and "adopted and produced results", and it is difficult for tool budgets to enter settlement.</Card>
  <Card title="settlement distortion" icon="triangle-alert">Incentives drift toward airdrop-style rules, point tasks, TVL—systematically misaligned with what PoB wants to measure.</Card>
  <Card title="No long-term network memory" icon="triangle-alert">Closed loops cannot be precipitated into composable reputation assets, and every collaboration on the network starts with zero trust.</Card>
</CardGrid>

## In one sentence, the positioning of the entire chain

PoW buys security, PoS buys economic consensus, and **PoB buys business reality**. WCN chose to tie scarce rewards to the third line, so PoB is the **soul layer** rather than the copywriting layer.

<Callout type="tip">
To evaluate whether WCN is "as serious as TradFi", you can look at PoB execution: rejection rate, quality of supplements, and whether dispute adjudication is open to iteration rules. The system is written in 8.1–8.5, and the credit is written in the Proof Desk routine.
</Callout>
