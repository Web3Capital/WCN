## Three load-bearing parts

WCN states its governance posture plainly. The network is founder-led. Strong direction is paired with two checks: an integrity threshold that can remove the founder, and a competence threshold that forces review. A short list of items stays outside any vote.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Eight-layer command stack" icon="layers">From L0 Constitution down to L7 service and standard nodes, each layer holds a defined function. Authority and responsibility sit on the same chain.</Card>
  <Card title="Founder accountability" icon="scale">Two thresholds. The integrity threshold can remove the founder under a very high bar. The competence threshold forces a public review when milestones are missed.</Card>
  <Card title="Six never-votable items" icon="lock">Privacy, AML, formed PoB records, signed agreements, absent minorities, and long-term sustainability cannot be overridden by majority vote.</Card>
</CardGrid>
