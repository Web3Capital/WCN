## What the City Partner holds

A City Partner is the partner-tier seat for one city. The network admits one per city, so the role is exclusive at the city level. Where the Country Partner coordinates a whole market, the City Partner owns local deal flow: it sources opportunities, runs local activity, and connects local capital and services on the ground.

The tier holds 150 to 300 seats across the network. The price runs from USD 20,000 to 100,000. As a partner tier, the seat binds rights to the same duties — resource introduction, business advancement, evidence submission, and compliance.
