## What are the region/city nodes?

City or regional nodes map the abstract global collaboration of WCN to concrete city clusters: e.g. Shenzhen-Hong Kong twin cities, Dubai-Riyadh corridor, Berlin encryption cluster. Its KPIs usually include **verifiable local events**, **stage advancement of introduced projects** and **number of local capital/service connections**, rather than online fan growth.

<Statement>Without city nodes, the network would easily remain stuck in cross-border Zoom and group emailing; PoB would also lack auxiliary evidence such as offline minutes, check-ins, and contract signing places.</Statement>
