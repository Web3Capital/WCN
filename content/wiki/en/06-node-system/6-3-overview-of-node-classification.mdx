# Six tiers, two classes. Partner tiers carry a governance seat; participation tiers do not.
</Hero>

<MetaGrid>
  <Meta label="Partner tiers" value="Founding, Country, City, Track — rights and duties bound to one NFT" />
  <Meta label="Participation tiers" value="Service Provider and Standard Node — service or open access, no governance seat" />
  <Meta label="Onchain standard" value="ERC-721 plus ERC-6551 (Token Bound Account) carries each node's account and record" />

## The four partner tiers

A partner-tier NFT is a partner contract. It couples rights — governance, revenue share, deal-flow priority — to responsibilities — resource introduction, business advancement, evidence submission, and compliance — on one credential.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Founding Partner" description="Up to 100 seats. USD 500,000. A permanent global governance seat, never re-issued. See 6.4." icon="landmark" />
  <Card title="Country Partner" description="50–65 seats. USD 50,000–500,000. One partner per country, exclusive coordination, 5–10% of that country's protocol-layer revenue. See 6.5." icon="flag" />
  <Card title="City Partner" description="150–300 seats. USD 20,000–100,000. One partner per city, owning local deal flow. See 6.6." icon="building-2" />
  <Card title="Track Partner" description="20–25 seats. USD 50,000–300,000. One partner per track, the cross-geography global representative. See 6.7." icon="layers" />
</CardGrid>

## The two participation tiers

Participation tiers provide capability or access. They hold no governance seat and carry no revenue share.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Service Provider" description="200–500 seats. USD 30,000. Professional-capability service nodes across 12 categories. See 6.9." icon="wrench" />
  <Card title="Standard Node" description="Open cap. USD 5,000. Open application for basic participation, on ERC-721 plus ERC-6551. See 6.9." icon="users" />
</CardGrid>

<Statement>The pyramid is the routing and accountability schema. A tier sets a node's rights, its duties, its price, and its standing on the Proof ledger — so every outcome can name who signed and in what role.</Statement>

## Country-tier pricing

The Country Partner price spans USD 50,000 to 500,000. A market's tier sets the figure within that range.

<Callout type="info">
The country schedule prices the Country Partner seat by market, not by partner. One country holds one Country Partner, so the figure also sets the cost of exclusive coordination in that market.
</Callout>
