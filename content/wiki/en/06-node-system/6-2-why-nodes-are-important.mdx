## The system weight of the node relative to the general user

Ordinary users contribute attention and communication; nodes contribute **routable resources, executable commitments and archivable evidence**. In TradFi, this corresponds to the difference between "institutional users on the terminal" and "anonymous market viewers": the latter does not incur underwriting, capital allocation or research responsibilities.

<Statement>For WCN, the key indicator is not "how many people see it", but "how many closed loops can fall into contract, delivery and PoB".</Statement>
