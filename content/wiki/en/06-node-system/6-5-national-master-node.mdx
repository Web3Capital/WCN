## What the Country Partner holds

A Country Partner is the partner-tier seat for one sovereign market. The network admits one per country, so the role is exclusive: a single accountable partner coordinates the country's node subgraph — its City Partners, its Track Partners, its service and standard nodes — into the global network.

The seat earns 5–10% of that country's protocol-layer revenue. The price runs from USD 50,000 to 500,000, set by the country pricing schedule in 6.3. The tier cap across all countries is 50 to 65 seats.

<Callout type="info">
In some markets a Country Partner may sit alongside a licensed entity: WCN provides the protocol and routing, and the local licensed party provides the compliance shell. The design separates the partner role from the regulated legal entity, so the title is not read as a regulatory endorsement.
</Callout>
