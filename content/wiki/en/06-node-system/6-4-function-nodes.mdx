## What the Founding Partner holds

A Founding Partner holds one of at most 100 permanent governance seats in WCN. The seat is global in scope, not tied to a country, a city, or a track. The price is USD 500,000. The supply is fixed: once the 100 seats are taken, the tier is never re-issued.

Like every partner tier, the seat is a partner contract on one NFT. It couples rights — governance, revenue share, deal-flow priority — to responsibilities — resource introduction, business advancement, evidence submission, and compliance. The Founding Partner sits at the top of that structure.

## How the partner tiers stack

The Founding Partner sits above the three coordinating partner tiers. Each lower tier holds a defined scope and a defined right within the same pyramid.

<Callout type="info">
A Founding Partner may also hold a coordinating seat — for example a Country or Track Partner — under a separate credential. The governance seat and the coordination role stay distinct, so rights and conflicts remain disclosed in the system.
</Callout>
