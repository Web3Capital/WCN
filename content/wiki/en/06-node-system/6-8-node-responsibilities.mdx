## Node Responsibility Structure

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="resource responsibility" description="Continuously introduce verifiable objects by application type: project one-pager, fund IC memo, service quotation, media schedule, etc. instead of exchanging empty business cards." icon="package" />
  <Card title="Advance responsibility" description="Respond to tasks within the agreed SLA: arrange meetings, promote signing, coordinate third-party delivery; reasons for delays need to be noted in the system." icon="zap" />
  <Card title="burden of proof" description="After closing the loop, cooperate to upload key pages of the contract, on-chain tx, invoices, and email thread summaries (in line with confidentiality classification); refusal to cooperate will be considered a breach of contract." icon="clipboard-list" />
  <Card title="system responsibility" description="Comply with authority, confidentiality classification, KYC sharing rules and review; national/regional nodes are also subject to the external rhetoric list." icon="scale" />
</CardGrid>

<Statement>If nodes only have badges but no obligations, WCN will degenerate into a LinkedIn group; PoB will also be unable to trigger settlement due to lack of evidence.</Statement>

## Behavioral Boundaries and Violation Types

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="False claims are prohibited" description="Results that are not signed, paid, or uploaded shall not be recorded as closed loop in advance; similar investment banks shall not falsely report book status." icon="ban" />
  <Card title="No ultra vires endorsement" description="In particular, national/regional nodes must not imply regulatory approval or WCN guarantee for products that do not exist." icon="ban" />
  <Card title="Shadow trading is prohibited" description="Completion outside the system without leaving traces will undermine distribution and auditing; major transactions should go through task and PoB channels." icon="ban" />
  <Card title="It is forbidden to occupy pits and produce no output" description="If the value is lower than the threshold for a long time, an observation period should be triggered or the seat should be ceded, and the route should be given to a node with higher output." icon="ban" />
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="tip">
Node economics suggests: **Explicit opportunity costs** - While occupying routing priority and brand exposure, disclose the quarterly minimum contribution threshold (number of projects introduced, number of closed loops, number of events) to align governance penalties with market expectations.
</Callout>
