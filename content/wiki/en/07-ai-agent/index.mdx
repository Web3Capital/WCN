<Callout type="tip">
A common misunderstanding in the industry is that "Agent" is equated with "a robot that surfs the Internet, places orders, and signs by itself." WCN does the opposite: Agents only perform authorized, replayable, and brakeable actions, and ultimately commitments and funds still fall on the governance chain of nodes and people.
</Callout>
