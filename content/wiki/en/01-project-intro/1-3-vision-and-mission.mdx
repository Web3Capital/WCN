## Vision Vision

> Become a global business network infrastructure that connects projects, capital, services, distribution, execution and asset carrying in the Web3 + AI era.

Three keywords:

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Global business network" icon="globe">It is not a small localized cooperation circle, but a network of nodes across countries, cities, and tracks.</Card>
  <Card title="Web3 + AI" icon="bot">Putting human nodes and AI Agents into the same execution and settlement framework is the uniqueness of WCN.</Card>
  <Card title="infrastructure" icon="building-2">The goal is not to do a round of transactions or an event, but to become a long-term reusable organizational method.</Card>
</CardGrid>

## Mission Mission

> Upgrade the resource flow that originally relied on acquaintance relationships, fragmented collaboration, and temporary matching into a business system that is verifiable, settleable, manageable, and scalable.

The core of the mission lies in "upgrading". It's not that there are no resources in the industry today, but that most of these resources exist in a low-structured way - people know people, temporary groups, temporary introductions, temporary cooperation, temporary review. Such a system can make a small number of transactions, but it is difficult to make a stable network.

## Four changes driven by WCN

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Network matching → network collaboration" icon="refresh-cw">Things that were originally accomplished by individual resources are gradually being accomplished by node networks and workflows.</Card>
  <Card title="Subjective claims → verifiable results" icon="circle-check">The assignment of value, which originally relied on verbal description, gradually became determined by evidence, results and attribution rules.</Card>
  <Card title="Artificial bottleneck → Human-machine collaboration" icon="zap">A large amount of research, engagement, promotion and collaboration work can be executed and amplified by Agents.</Card>
  <Card title="Short-term cooperation → long-term precipitation" icon="trending-up">One-time cooperation is no longer just a one-time transaction, but can be accumulated into node reputation, PoB record and long-term position.</Card>
</CardGrid>

## long term outcome

The ultimate goal of WCN is to make the real business network itself an infrastructure that can continue to operate, continue to settle, and continue to expand.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="node">Not just a contact person, but a responsible unit.</Card>
  <Card title="Agent">Not just tools, but execution units.</Card>
  <Card title="PoB">It’s not just about keeping score, it’s about the results.</Card>
  <Card title="Settlement">It’s not just about dividing accounts, it’s about value accumulation.</Card>
  <Card title="chain">Not just a technical carrier, but a trusted bearer layer.</Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="tip">
A project without a vision can easily end up with only partial actions; a system without a mission can easily end up with only superficial expansion.
</Callout>
