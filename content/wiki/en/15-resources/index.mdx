## Each material carries one job

The five materials divide the work cleanly. The whitepaper holds the complete argument and the canonical definitions. The One-Pager and Pitch Deck manage attention under time pressure. The FAQ answers recurring questions. The Legal and Risk Statement separates information from commitment.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Match the material to the moment" description="Use the Pitch Deck for a financing meeting, the One-Pager for a first email or event introduction, the whitepaper for due diligence, the FAQ for repeated questions, and the Legal and Risk Statement for anything that touches a claim." />
  <Step title="Match the framing to the reader" description="Investors read for unit economics, stages, and risk. Strategic partners read for interfaces and responsibility boundaries. Node candidates read for access, obligations, and Proof of Business." />
  <Step title="Keep every channel aligned" description="Any PDF or link sent externally must agree with the wiki. A major change updates the whitepaper version and the FAQ at the same time." />
</Steps>
