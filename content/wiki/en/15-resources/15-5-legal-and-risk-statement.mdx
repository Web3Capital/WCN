## General information; not an offer or solicitation

WCN materials published through its websites, wiki, whitepaper, presentation files, and communities are for **general information and education only**. They do not constitute an offer to sell, an invitation to subscribe, or a solicitation to buy or sell any security, fund share, derivative, deposit, or other regulated financial product in any jurisdiction. Any decision to invest or participate must rest on complete information you obtain, and must be made as applicable law permits. During Phase 1 and Phase 2, the protocol is not open to US Persons.

<Callout type="warning">If you are in a jurisdiction that restricts distributing this information to anyone other than certain investors, stop accessing the materials and consult legal counsel.</Callout>

## Digital assets, NFTs, and onchain activity

Where the network involves a Token, a Node NFT, or another onchain carrier, the description covers proposed or current design functions only. Such carriers may be classified as securities, virtual assets, payment instruments, or another regulated category in a given jurisdiction. **Classification depends on the facts and the jurisdiction, and is determined by external legal counsel.** Transfer, pledge, or secondary-market trading may be restricted by contract or prohibited by law. You must assess legality yourself; WCN does not guarantee that any carrier can be lawfully held or traded in your jurisdiction.

## Technology, market, and operational risk (non-exhaustive)

Participating in or relying on the WCN ecosystem may carry risks including, but not limited to: **smart contract or software defects**, third-party infrastructure failure, key or operational security incidents, insufficient market liquidity, partner default, regulatory change, and business failure if network effects do not form. These risks may cause **partial or total loss of value**. The risk disclosures in the materials do not purport to list every risk.
