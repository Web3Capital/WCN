Participating in or relying on the WCN ecosystem may face risks including but not limited to: **Smart contract or software defects**, **Third-party infrastructure failure**, **Key or operational security incidents**, **Insufficient market liquidity**, **Partner default**, **Regulatory policy changes**, **Business failure caused by failure to form network effects**.
