## What a single page contains

The One-Pager uses fixed information blocks. A consistent structure lets a reader compare WCN against other materials at a glance.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="One-sentence positioning" icon="pin">What network WCN is, who it serves, and how it differs from a single platform — stated in testable terms, not adjectives.</Card>
  <Card title="Problem and timing" icon="timer">Why current structures fail on information, execution, and trust, and why now is the window for networked collaboration.</Card>
  <Card title="Solution and form" icon="puzzle">Nodes, the three ledgers, Proof of Business, and agents — each on one line, at a level a non-technical reader can follow.</Card>
  <Card title="Stage and signals" icon="trending-up">The current stage and the signals that can be disclosed. Unverified items are marked as in progress, not implied as done.</Card>
  <Card title="How to engage" icon="door-open">Distinct contact paths for investors, strategic partners, and Verification Node candidates — not a single general address.</Card>
  <Card title="Compliance pointer" icon="scale">A short line at the foot pointing to the Legal and Risk Statement, without taking space from the body.</Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Before sending" description="Confirm the figures and stage language match the current whitepaper and deck. Verify the logo, domain, and contact address are official channels." />
  <Step title="When sending" description="Summarize the page in three to five sentences in the email body. Name the file consistently so the recipient can archive it." />
  <Step title="When following up" description="If the reader asks for depth, send the whitepaper link and offer a meeting with the deck. Avoid repeating the pitch before the long read." />
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">The One-Pager is not a shrunken whitepaper. Shrinking the text only makes it unreadable. Remove the arguments and keep the conclusions and the citations.</Callout>

## Limits and division of labor

<Statement>The One-Pager does not carry PoB mechanics, the legal classification of any Token or Node NFT, or compliance conclusions for a specific jurisdiction. Those belong to the whitepaper and to a legal advisor.</Statement>
