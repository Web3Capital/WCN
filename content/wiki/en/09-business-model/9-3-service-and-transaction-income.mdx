## Service delivery

The Service Provider tier holds professional service nodes across defined categories — legal, security, growth, research, branding, and related capabilities. When a project node draws on these capabilities, the work enters the network's record and proceeds toward a verified outcome.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Legal and structuring" icon="scale">Architecture design, compliance path, and contract review. Work is scoped to a defined deliverable and recorded for reconciliation.</Card>
  <Card title="Security and audit" icon="shield">Contract audit, operational security, and architecture review. High value, low frequency, bound to outcome milestones.</Card>
  <Card title="Growth and brand" icon="trending-up">Business development, media, and content. Scoped to defined milestones so the work maps to a verifiable result.</Card>
  <Card title="Research and data" icon="search">Diligence, track mapping, and benchmarking, delivered as a defined report or scoped engagement.</Card>
</CardGrid>

## Two buckets in the Carry waterfall

Service and coordination work is settled through the Carry waterfall, not through a separate fee schedule. Two buckets carry it. Both are paid from net profit, after backers' capital is returned.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Sourcing and Coordination · 10%" icon="handshake">The share for introducing the opportunity and coordinating the work that advanced it. It rewards moving a contact into a structured outcome.</Card>
  <Card title="Service Provider Pool · 5%" icon="wrench">The share for service delivery on the outcome. It converts scoped, accepted work into a settled portion of net profit.</Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="info">
Both buckets distribute net profit, defined as profit after backers' capital is returned. Settlement follows PoB reconciliation, and settled records cannot be altered after the fact.
</Callout>

## The order of settlement

A clear order keeps attribution disciplined. Income is recognized when the outcome is verified, not when contact is made.

<Steps>
  <Step number={1} title="Define the outcome">State in writing what counts as the result — a signing, a transfer, a launch, a delivery, or a KPI met.</Step>
  <Step number={2} title="Submit evidence">Contracts, receipts, onchain records, partner endorsement, and reviewable logs support the claim.</Step>
  <Step number={3} title="Reconcile through PoB">A Verification Node forms once reconciliation holds, which makes the outcome Verified.</Step>
  <Step number={4} title="Settle the shares">Net profit is distributed through the waterfall, and the two service buckets settle their portions.</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">
Node equity can start the network, but service and coordination income proves it conducts real business. Without verified delivery, a seat is only a position with nothing settled behind it.
</Callout>
