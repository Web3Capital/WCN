## Access: opportunities and lower friction

The founder seeds Day-0 supply from eleven years of deal flow, so a node finds real opportunities on entry. Access turns scattered sourcing into one structured queue.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Structured Deal Flow" icon="refresh-cw">Opportunities arrive in one data model, which compresses scattered sourcing into a systematic queue with agent summaries.</Card>
  <Card title="Orchestrated tasks" icon="handshake">Service and distribution nodes find the right counterpart faster. A task-level scope and acceptance standard reduces the chance of dispute.</Card>
  <Card title="Nomination priority" icon="clipboard-list">A node holds nomination priority — subject to seat cap and KPIs — for joint roadshows and matchmaking, distinct from open public channels.</Card>
  <Card title="A verifiable endorsement" icon="medal">The audited WCN-node mark, used under brand rules, serves as a verifiable endorsement that lowers a counterparty's initial trust cost.</Card>
</CardGrid>

## Position: collaboration that carries forward

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Network-level signal" icon="radio">Aggregated track interest, capital-preference shifts, and service bottlenecks, drawn from supply and demand inside the network.</Card>
  <Card title="Reusable collaboration" icon="globe">A verified combination of nodes can be reused across deals, which shortens the path to outcome on later projects.</Card>
</CardGrid>

As the node base grows, scarcity rises within a segment. A node that performs early holds higher weight in matching, joint activity, and incentive pools, under the governing documents.

## Settlement: a citable contribution record

A node's verified outcome enters the record through Proof of Business (PoB). Only a verifiable, traceable, reviewed outcome becomes a verification node on the Proof Ledger and reaches settlement.

<Statement>The largest long-term benefit is a stable, citable contribution record in PoB and the Carry waterfall — a third-party-verifiable history that supports later rounds, mergers, and joint funds.</Statement>

## Value is not credited automatically

<Callout type="warning">A node that fails to deliver, refuses evidence, or triggers a compliance event faces reduced authorization, a reset of incentive weight, or exit. The position is the option; execution is how the option is exercised.</Callout>

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Output" icon="zap">An advanced deal, a task delivered on time, a capital action, or an equivalent verified outcome.</Card>
  <Card title="Symmetric duty" icon="scroll">Wider authorization is matched by more frequent disclosure and review.</Card>
  <Card title="PoB threshold" icon="circle-check">Entering settlement requires a reviewed evidence package and no open dispute.</Card>
  <Card title="Compound condition" icon="trending-up">Only sustained performance across quarters opens the fast path for upgrade and renewal.</Card>
</CardGrid>
