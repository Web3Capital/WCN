## Six tiers, two kinds of seat

The pyramid holds six tiers. The first four bind rights and responsibility in one contract. The last two cover professional service and open participation.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Partner tiers (NFT binds rights to responsibility)" icon="handshake">Founding, Country, City, and Track. The NFT binds governance, revenue share, and Deal Flow priority to four duties: bring resources, advance collaboration, submit evidence, comply. Each partner NFT is a partner contract.</Card>
  <Card title="Participation tiers (service and open access)" icon="users">Service Provider covers twelve classes of professional capability. Standard is open application for base participation. Standard uses ERC-721 plus ERC-6551, so each node carries its own account and performance record.</Card>
</CardGrid>
