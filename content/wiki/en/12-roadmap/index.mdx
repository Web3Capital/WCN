<Hero badge="Chapter 12 · Roadmap" lead="The Web3 Capital Network (WCN) roadmap runs in three phases — Business Network, Systematization, and Decentralization — across M0 to M36 and beyond. Each phase is milestone-gated: it exits only when its criteria are met, not when the calendar turns.">
# Prove the business network first. Add the Token and onchain settlement only after the evidence holds.
</Hero>

<MetaGrid>
  <Meta label="Core themes" value="Three phases, milestone-gated, plus the first 90 days" />
  <Meta label="Method" value="Business first, Token later — gated by exit criteria" />
  <Meta label="Audience" value="Prospective nodes, partners, and reviewers" />
</MetaGrid>

<Callout type="info">A phase advances only when its exit criteria hold. The Token arrives in Phase 3, after cross-jurisdiction compliance, not earlier. These are plans, not guarantees, and they describe intended sequence rather than promised outcomes.</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Phase 1 · Business Network">M0–M12. Sign the first 100 Founding Partners, register nodes, and close the first Proof of Business (PoB). Exit: 30+ Founding nodes and 5+ PoB records.</Step>
  <Step title="Phase 2 · Systematization">M12–M24. Roll out Country, City, and Track partners and standardize operations. Exit: 200+ nodes and a documented Token decision.</Step>
  <Step title="Phase 3 · Decentralization">M24–M36+. Introduce the Token, complete cross-jurisdiction compliance, and transition toward a DAO. Exit: 1,000+ nodes and five-jurisdiction compliance.</Step>
</Steps>
